Consoles
Consoles are gaming systems used by millions across the world. There have been many different kinds of consoles, made by different companies. The main consoles of today are made by Microsoft, Sony, and Nintendo. Microsoft makes the Xbox, Sony makes Playstation, and Nintendo makes the Wii system. Games for consoles are more often than not physical disks, however, nowadays thanks to technology, they can be purchased and downloaded from the comfort of your home.
