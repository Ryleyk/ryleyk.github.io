Subgenres

    There are many different styles of hardcore.

  • There is the traditional approach, with simple power chords called Youth Crew.
  • There is also the newer, more intimidating, Beatdown with low tunings, slow tempos, and inhumane heaviness.
  • Another, fairly new style is Metalcore,which is very technical, and much more distasteful to one that knows nothing about hardcore.
  • Finally, there is Crossover which is played at breakneck speed. Crossover is a style of the genre that is fused with thrash metal, like Metallica or Slayer.
